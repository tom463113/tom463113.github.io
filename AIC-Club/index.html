But soon, the senior co-founder was transfered to other school.

Building my first drone

For a while, there was little activity in our club after our beloved senior transfered. I realized that if this continued, we might become just another inactive group, like many other “paper clubs” in my school.

I began building my first drone partly because a friend gave me three drone motors after he broke one of the four. The second reason was that I accidentally met a very good friend who also played FPV around that time. After that, I purchased all the necessary components online and started assembling them.

It was the second time I use the soldering iron, so the welding spots was awful.

figuring out the wiring

After a tons of bugs and troubles, the drone could lift off finally without burning any electronic components or chips.

very cool right? It only cost me 500rmb (≈71usd)
